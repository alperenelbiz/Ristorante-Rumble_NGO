# 🍝 Ristorante Rumble

> *Cook, compete, and conquer — welcome to the ultimate culinary showdown!*

## 📖 About

**Ristorante Rumble** is a fast-paced multiplayer cooking and combat game set in a world inspired by Italian mafia films.  
During the day, teams manage their restaurants, cook dishes, and serve customers to earn money and reputation.  
When night falls, a fierce battle begins — players use their earnings to arm themselves and sabotage rival restaurants to dominate the culinary underworld!

## 🎮 Gameplay

### Day Phase
- Prepare and serve dishes to real and fake customers.
- Earn money and reputation points by running your restaurant efficiently.
- Purchase better ingredients, equipment, or sabotage options from the shop.

### Night Phase
- Spend your earnings on weapons and upgrades.
- Attack rival restaurants to steal reputation points.
- Defend your own safe while launching strategic assaults.

Victory depends on balancing business success and nighttime sabotage — can you out-cook and outfight your competition?

## 🛠️ Technologies

- Unity 2022.3.7f1
- Netcode for GameObjects (NGO)
- Custom multiplayer game logic
- Server-client architecture with dedicated server support

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Host or join a multiplayer session.
2. Manage your restaurant during the day:
   - Take customer orders.
   - Prepare meals using cooking stations.
   - Serve customers before they get angry!
3. Visit the shop to buy weapons, fake customers, or upgrades.
4. Defend or attack during the night phase:
   - Coordinate with your team.
   - Sabotage enemy kitchens.
   - Protect your own assets!
